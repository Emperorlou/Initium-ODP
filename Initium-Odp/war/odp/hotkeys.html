The following hotkeys exist:

  • H - this opens this page.
  • E - this explores your current location.
  • W - this explores your current location while ignoring old combat sites/destroyed camps.
  • V - display nearby items.
  • B - display nearby character
  • M - open the player-made map.
  • N - creates a popup of the local navigation.
  • F - this deletes and forgets a combat site - usable only from within an old site.
  • G - collect gold from a dead corpse.
  • R - this rests if you're at a rest location
  • R - this will create a combat site if you're at a location at supports them.
  • I - open your inventory
  • O - open the game settings page.
  • Q - open your quest log.
  • P - open your profile.
  • S - open a list of nearby merchants.
  • G - open your guard settings.
  • X - open the invention window.
  • C - open the character switcher.
  • Alt + U - move up a character.
  • ALt + J - move down a character.
  • Alt + K - rotate through characters that are both on your account and in your party.
  • Alt + L - switch to the leader of your party if it's on the same account as you.
